About Web Invader

Web Invader is a browser arcade game from BNC Arcade, built for quick sessions, score chasing, and global leaderboard competition. The service is operated by dr. plan.

What It Is

The game blends classic invader-style action with staged enemy formations, special monsters, a final boss, keyboard controls, touch controls, and online score saving.

Current Status

Web Invader is an independently operated web game under the BNC Arcade Web Invader brand. The service may evolve with daily challenges, expanded rankings, achievements, and carefully placed advertising or sponsorship support.
