How To Play

Defeat every regular enemy and special monster, survive the boss stage, and save your score to the leaderboard.

Keyboard

  • Move with the left and right arrow keys.
  • Fire with the space bar.
  • Pause with P or the Pause button.

Touch

  • Hold the screen and drag left or right to move.
  • While holding, tap with another finger to fire.
  • Dodge bombs from special monsters and the final boss.

Scoring

  • Regular enemies award steady points.
  • Special monsters award larger bonuses.
  • The final boss awards a time-based bonus.
