Information We Collect

Web Invader stores player names and scores when a player chooses to save a record. The site also records aggregate visit counts using server-side request information to prevent duplicate daily counts.

Local Storage

The game may use browser local storage for local score history, cached visit stats, and game preferences. You can clear this data through your browser settings.

Advertising

If advertising is enabled, third party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this site or other sites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Other ad networks may provide their own opt-out choices.

Service Providers

Web Invader uses Supabase for leaderboard and visit-count infrastructure and may use hosting or analytics providers to operate and improve the service.

For privacy questions or data removal requests, use the contact page.
